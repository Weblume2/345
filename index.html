Our Story

Pawfect Bites was created by dog lovers who wanted healthier options for their furry family members. Tired of reading labels full of unpronounceable chemicals, we started baking in our kitchen.


Every recipe is carefully crafted with real ingredients and no artificial junk. Because they aren't just pets, they're family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your treats grain-free?
Yes! We offer a wide range of grain-free options like our Sweet Potato Chews and Beef Nuggets.
Are they safe for puppies?
Absolutely. We have specific 'Puppy Training Minis' designed for smaller teeth and sensitive tummies.
How long do they stay fresh?
Since we use no preservatives, they last about 6 weeks in a cool, dry place. Or freeze them for up to 3 months!
Do you offer refunds?
If your pup doesn't love them, let us know within 30 days and we'll refund your order. No questions asked.
